The Back Yard

Resturant Branding and Website

To Design a Brand to Match a Hip and Young Neighborhood for a New Bar with a beloved History

We were asked to design a logo and website to reflex a neighborhood bar near the  B&O Railroad Museum,  this neighborhood was a working-class Irish neighborhood in it's past history in Baltimore, Maryland but now is growing with young modern working class of all backgrounds. The owners wanted an elevated brand not just a bar look but the experience with wine education and interesting beer selections but still match the history of the area and play on the B&0 Railroad Museum across form the bar. The logo for the bar needed to be professional and also have a hipster logo vib but not age with the end of that hipster trend.

Logo Design + Website Design

The First Drafts

We developed many drafts for the project and wanted to express the railroad theme but not in a litteraly way.  The client liked the Railroad spikes for an element so we experimented with the theme.

The Final Look

With this design, we were inspired by railroad spikes and used traditional typography to make it ageless. The design was approved and we moved on to the web design. 

We were asked to design a Hot Sauce bottle with the new logo element but called The Bone Yard, which was tons of fun.
