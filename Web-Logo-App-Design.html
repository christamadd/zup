Helse and the "Ossler" 

Our task was to create an impactful brand identity for this innovative product and design a website that would accurately reflect the essence of the company. Additionally, we prepared all the necessary materials to attract potential funding for the venture.

Although, regrettably, the start-up did not proceed as planned—a common occurrence in the entrepreneurial world—we take pride in our solid work and the enjoyable experience of crafting a brand from inception to completion. We also take satisfaction in developing a website that effectively conveyed the start-up's vision, assisting them in visualizing their aspirations.

Logo + Website + App Design

Design Drafts for the Helse Brand

What does Helse mean in Norwegian? HEALTH!

We aimed to create a logo that embodies a modern aesthetic while avoiding a cold impression, capturing the essence of a European vibe with a Norwegian touch. Presented below are our initial design attempts.

The Final Logo

Clean and Minimalistic Design: Inspired by Swiss design and known for its simplicity and clarity. The logo features clean lines, geometric shapes, and a minimalist approach, avoiding unnecessary details or embellishments. This design approach reflects the precision and efficiency associated with the Helse device.
