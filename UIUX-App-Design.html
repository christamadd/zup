App Design

RESEARCH | BRAIN STORMING | ​ DESIGN | ANALYSIS

Mako Medical

The Ask: To design an interface for a tech medical app for the web and phone. The interface must walk the patient through the test and be simple to use with a way to record the results for the tester. 

Research and Background: The Voice Check diagnostic test, which runs on a smartphone or computer, asks the user to count from 50 to 70. The audio is translated into a visual representation of their voice, a spectrogram, made up of 512 features vocal biomarkers.  The app applies artificial intelligence to compare the spectrogram to a composite image from the voice of many people proven to have COVID-19.

The Problem & Solution

To design a prototype that can mimic the future real app so design issues and user flows can be evaluated and tuned to make an app to help people navagate a test and possible illiness. 

We used Adobe XD to design the prototype and used its animation features to simulate how the actual app could work and in doing so discover its touchpoints weather good or bad.  We also made a web based version of the app. 

Once the app was tested and approved we were asked to make the app store advertising for the product.
