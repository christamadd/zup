Imagination 
is a Octopus 

As a designer, I'm constantly thinking about creativity - what it is, where it comes from, how to tap into it. One night, I had the strangest dream: my hair became an octopus, tentacles spiraling and swimming around my head while I was trying to talk about creativity. When I woke up, I realized my brain had just shown me the perfect metaphor.

The octopus was the message. Creativity moves like this: fluid, multidirectional, impossible to pin down. It possesses numerous tentacles, each one exploring different depths, different crevices of thought. It slips through our rational minds, finding connections we didn't know existed. Some tentacles reach toward the light, others dive deeper into darkness. All of them are thinking, feeling, creating simultaneously.

At zeroUp, that's exactly what we do - we help your ideas swim, reach, and explore possibilities you didn't know existed.

Christa Madden  |  PRESIDENT OF ZEROUP MEDIA
